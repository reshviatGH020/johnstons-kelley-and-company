About Us

JK's & Company is an accounting company in Colombo, Sri Lanka. We are a leading provider of Auditing, Taxation and Company secretarial work. Our Key Services

Auditing of Financial Statement

At JK's & Company, we provide meticulous auditing services to ensure the accuracy and reliability of your financial statements, giving you peace of mind and compliance with regulatory standards.

Review Engagements

Review engagements provide limited assurance on financial statements through a detailed analysis, inquiry, and analytical procedures. This offers a cost-effective alternative to a full audit.

Engagements on Agreed Upon Audit Procedures

Engagements on Agreed Upon Audit Procedures provide clients with a tailored approach to audit specific areas of their financial information, offering flexibility and targeted insights.

CSR & ESG Audits

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) and ESG audits at JK's & Company focus on evaluating a company's social and environmental impact, ensuring transparency and adherence to sustainability goals.
